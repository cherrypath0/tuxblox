# System Requirements

The following are the minimum system requirements for running TuxBlox. 

For the best experience, keep your Linux distribution, kernel, and graphics drivers up to date.

### Hardware Requirements
* **Processor:** Intel or AMD, 64-bit x86 (x86_64) Architecture with SSE3 Support
* **Memory:** DDR3 4 GB or higher
* **Storage:** 1 GB or higher (SSD recommended)

### Software Requirements
* **Operating System:** Any major 64-bit Linux distribution
* **Kernel Version:** 5.0 or higher
* **Filesystem:** An unencrypted filesystem (because encrypted filesystems slows down the loading process of our binaries)
